## FAQ

**Do I need the Pro version if I already use cursor-doctor free?**

The free version gives you `scan`, `lint`, and `check`. You can see what's broken. Pro gives you unlimited `fix`, `audit`, `conflicts`, `test`, and `team drift`. You can auto-repair issues, detect conflicts across files, test rule effectiveness with AI, and track team config drift.

If you're maintaining rules for a team or want to automate fixes, Pro saves hours.

**Can I use these templates with the free version of cursor-doctor?**

Yes. The templates work with any version of cursor-doctor. Pro unlocks unlimited auto-fix and advanced diagnostics, but the templates themselves are just `.mdc` files. Copy them into your project and use them with the free CLI.

**Do the templates work with GitHub Copilot or other AI assistants?**

Most templates are Cursor-specific (MDC format with globs and alwaysApply). Three templates (in the AI Tools category) are designed for cross-tool compatibility: `copilot-instructions.mdc`, `claude-md.mdc`, and `cross-tool-config.mdc`.

If you use Copilot, you can adapt the instructions from any template into a `.github/copilot-instructions.md` file. The practices and patterns transfer. The format is different.
